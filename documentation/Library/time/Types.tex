The following table lists the non-modifiable type
synonyms along with the corresponding modifiable type.

\table{c|r@{\qquad\qquad}||@{\qquad\qquad}c|l}

\tt{<time._>} & \tt{<time.h>} & \tt{<time._>} & \multicolumn{1}{c}{\tt{<time.h>}}\\

\hline

\type{Size}  & \tt{size_t}  & \type{Timespec} & \tt{struct timespec}\\

\type{Clock} & \tt{clock_t} & \type{Tm}       & \tt{struct tm}\\

\type{Time}  & \tt{time_t}\\

\elbat

Additionally, if both macros \tt{__STDC_LIB_EXT1__} and
\tt{__STDC_WANT_LIB_EXT1__} remain defined before including \tt{<time._>},
then the synonyms \type{Errno} and \type{Rsize} are also defined,
corresponding to the types \tt{errno_t} and \tt{rsize_t}.
